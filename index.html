How to Play

Choose your options and start a new game. You can play with up to four players. Tap or click on cards to flip them over. Find pairs of cards to make a match and score a point. Whoever get the most match points is the winner! When cards are matched, click or tap them to see the picture and learn the English and Chinese words. Enjoy yourself!
